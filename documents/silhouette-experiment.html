Silhouette Photo Experiment

Here's an experimental silhouette photograph that I took of my wife, 38 weeks pregnant.

This is my first attempt at a silhouette photograph. I'm pretty proud of it. The light shining around her neck is natural sunlight. There was a little post processing, but it was mostly touch ups and adding the brownish hue. I was able to capture the silhouette by underexposing the photo and speeding up the shutter.
